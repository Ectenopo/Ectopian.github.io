The Lore of the Universe

Yami is a huge being that creates these creatures, and Yami has been frozen in space for a millennium.

There are multiple races of human species. No, I'm not talking about black and white, I'm talking about... human. No special stuff, just intelligent.

  • Necroptian: Lives for 5,000 years maximum. Shapeshifter (boar).
  • Actroptian: Lives for 50,000 years maximum. Demon (goat horns, sharp teeth).
  • Galoptian: Lives for 45,000 years maximum. Shapeshifter (fairy).
  • Dragonian: Lives for 1,000,000 years maximum. Demon, shapeshifter (dragon, sharp teeth, no goat horns).
  • Ectopian: Lives for 500,000 years maximum. Fluent (has its own language and automatically translates other languages and learns them).
